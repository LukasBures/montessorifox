About Me

Hello, I'm Kamila, an AMI Certified Montessori Guide (3–6) and Positive Discipline Certified Parent Educator. I am also a mom who homeschools her children with love, respect, and curiosity every day.

I believe that every child has an inner drive to learn and grow when given freedom within clear boundaries and a well-prepared environment. My mission is to support families in nurturing independence, responsibility, critical thinking, and life-long love for learning.

I design printable materials, writing guides, and share practical tips that parents can easily use at home. All my creations are rooted in authentic Montessori principles and inspired by my own homeschooling journey.
